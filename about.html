My name is Brian Duncan, and I'm a developer.

But enough talking about myself in third person. I've stayed in Texas for most of my life. Born and raised here. It's the place that I call home, and it's the place where I've made the best of friends and memories.
I give most of my credit to my dad for my love of technology and programming. As well as my mom. They were always there to support me in whatever hobbies I decided to take up during the course of my life.
And because of them, and because of continued support from my friends, here I am. Trying my best to show my skills to the world, and to support others, like the people I love that always supported me.
So, to whoever sees my portfolio, and is interested in working with me, I thank you for joining me on my adventure. I'm passionate and driven to see anything through that has been laid in front of me. And I'll be excited working with you. Thank you.
